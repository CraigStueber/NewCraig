The First Game I made that made me fall in love with Programming.

Color Game

A simple RGB color game which was my very first JavaScript project. It was also the project that made me realize just how much I love front-end engineering. RGB is the light color spectrum, Red Green and Blue. At the top a random color is shown in the RGB form. {example RGB(255,49,65)} The goal of the game is to guess the color the numbers makes.
