Hi! My name is Craig

Let me tell you a bit about myself. Having degrees in both Illustration at VCU & Software Engineering at Strayer (finishing top of my class), I have brought both passions together. With a strong skill set in Object Oriented Programming, specializing in Java and Javascript, I can create dynamic programs and web applications. A family man at heart, all of my ambitions are driven in the betterment of our lives. A native of Richmond VA, my wife and I own a house in Randolph, where we spend our days enjoying the city we love so much.

Artist & Web Developer

I spent the first 15 years of my professional life as a wedding photographer and bartender. This has helped me excel at the soft skills that so many developers lack in today's market. Having had gone to an online school I learned just how important time management is, and learned to plan out everything, which not only has helped me in my professional life, but also in my personal life.

My love for problem solving is what brought me to Software Engineering.

On weekends my family and I enjoy going hiking and camping at any of the wonderful parks around Virginia. I like filling my free time during the week with painting models, playing Dungeon and Dragons along with other board games, and going for walks around the city.
