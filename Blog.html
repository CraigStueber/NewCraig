Week One

Oct 10, 2020

I was very nervous about starting my Master's Program at Strayer. When I ordered my text books for my one class, seeing there were 3 books made me even more scared. But after I started my class this week I relaxed a bit, realizing it was just another class. So Monday morning I woke up at 5am (which is the time I always wake up) and jumped right into my week. I set my weekly goals.

  • Get all my work at Kurb Media done
  • Watch my Project Management Videos
  • Read the assigned Chapters
  • Write out my discussion question
  • Apply for at least 10 freelance jobs on Upwork
  • Redo my personal web site
  • Write this post
With a list this large I was worried about getting it all done, but one thing I have learned about myself is that the more that is put on my plate the better I perform. So I set myself to the task first thing Monday morning. I turned on one of my favorite television shows, Scrubs, and just start knocking everything out. While working 12 hour days, I still found the time to go for walks with my wife, run a Dungeon and Dragon's game on Thursday night, and play a couple games of tennis with the family. Keeping focused and breaking down tasks into smaller components keeps things from getting overwhelming.

Thank you for reading,

Craig A Stueber
